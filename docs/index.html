About Us

Based in Durango, Colorado, Nomadic Off-Grid Solutions was born from a passion for adventure and a deep expertise in off-grid power systems. We understand the unique challenges of life on the road because we live it ourselves.

We specialize in designing and installing custom solar and electrical systems for RVs, campervans, Sprinters, Promasters, skoolies, and tiny homes on wheels. Every build is different, and we tailor each system to match your energy needs, vehicle layout, and travel style.

From weekend warriors to full-time nomads, we've helped adventurers across the Four Corners region gain energy independence. Our goal is simple: give you the power to go anywhere and stay as long as you want.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many solar panels do I need?

It depends on your energy usage and how you travel. A typical campervan setup ranges from 200W to 600W, while larger RVs may need 800W or more. We start every project with a personalized energy audit to size your system correctly.

What battery type is best for my setup?

We recommend lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries for most builds. They're lighter, last longer (3,000+ cycles), charge faster, and can be discharged deeper than AGM batteries. We'll help you choose the right option for your budget and needs.

How long does installation take?

Most installations take 1–3 days depending on the complexity of the system. A basic solar-and-battery setup can often be completed in a single day, while full electrical system builds may take longer.

Can I install solar on a curved roof?

Yes. We use flexible solar panels and custom mounting solutions for curved roofs on vans and fiberglass RVs. We'll assess your roof and recommend the best panel type and mounting approach.

Do you offer mobile or on-site installation?

We do. We primarily serve the Durango, CO area and surrounding Four Corners region, and we can come to your location for installation. Contact us to discuss scheduling and availability.

What warranty do you provide?

All of our installations come with a workmanship warranty, and the components we use carry their own manufacturer warranties. We stand behind our work and are always available for follow-up support.
